The ProClean System

Purified water.
Genuinely spot-free.

Tap water leaves spots — minerals dry on the glass. Our ProClean System pushes 100% purified, mineral-free water through a carbon-fibre pole, so your windows rinse perfectly clear and dry with zero streaks or spots, even on the upper floors — all from the safety of the ground.

  • No ladders leaning on your home
  • No detergent residue, no streaks
  • Reaches 2nd & 3rd-storey glass safely
  • Eco-friendly — just pure water
